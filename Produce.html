CCC 181 Application Development & Emerging Tech

Information

The course will review the concepts for systematically establishing, defining and managing the requirements for an application, from technical, organizational and management perspectives. The course will demonstrate emerging technologies, tools, methods, and/or techniques in application development. The course will involve building models concerning both functional and nonfunctional requirements/specification.
